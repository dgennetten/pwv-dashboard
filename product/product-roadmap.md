# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Activity Dashboard
The primary view — a configurable snapshot of patrol activity across the organization or for an individual member. Supports switchable time ranges (7 days, 1 month, 1 quarter, 1 year, all-time) and toggles between org-wide and per-member views. Headline stats include total patrols, trails covered, trees cleared, hikers contacted, and parking lot counts. The default logged-in view shows the current member's own stats.

### 2. Trail Health
A per-trail deep-dive into patrol coverage and trail condition data. Each trail shows patrol frequency, cumulative hiker contacts, trees down and cleared (by size class), violations recorded, maintenance work logged, and a computed patrol efficiency score. Trails can be filtered by geographic area, wilderness status, and difficulty. Flags under-patrolled trails relative to their visitor volume.

### 3. Leaderboards + Trends
Gamified contribution rankings and long-range trend charts. Leaderboards rank members by patrol count, volunteer hours, miles covered, and trees cleared — filterable by time range and area. Trend charts show org-wide patrol activity over time, violation rates by category, seasonal trail usage patterns, and year-over-year comparisons.
